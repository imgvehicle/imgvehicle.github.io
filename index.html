About imgVEHLCLE.com

Few words about this project

This wasn't just an idea. I had some issues with my BMW E60. Fuel pump was not working. My mechanic told me that I need to buy new one. No man, that's a lot of money! I bought new pump, but my car was still not working!

I wanted to check my fuses, but I could not locate correct fuse. In BMW car owner's manual there's no any description of the fuses

After 3 hours of looking for fuses, I found it! Thank you http://www.bimmerfest.com/

Let's do it!

Hey, that would be useful to make an website with all the specific information for car owners! Wiring diagrams, fuse box locations and meaning, lights description.

Creating website was easy, but not easy and time-consuming was gathering all the information. Thankfully there's a great community around imgvehicle.com where people help each other. We have 1500 quality posts, 50 active users and about 1000 unique visitors who are looking for help with their cars. That's a big achivement after 5 months and I hope it will grow constantly!
